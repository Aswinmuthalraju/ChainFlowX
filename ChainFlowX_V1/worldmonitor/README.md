# ChainFlowX 🌐  
**The Supply Chain Contagion Intelligence Engine**  

ChainFlowX is a real-time intelligence platform designed to map, track, and predict global supply chain contagions. Forked from the WorldMonitor geopolitical intelligence stack, ChainFlowX refocuses core OSINT, maritime, and infrastructure monitoring architectures toward trade resilience and logistics risk.  

---

## 🛠 CORE CAPABILITIES  

### 🧊 Maritime & Port Intelligence  
- **Real-Time AIS Tracking:** Interactive 3D globe visualization of global shipping flows.  
- **Bottleneck Monitoring:** Live congestion alerts for critical chokepoints (Suez, Panama, Hormuz, Malacca).  
- **Dark Vessel Detection:** Identification of AIS-disabled vessels in high-risk zones.  

### ⚡ Infrastructure & Utilities  
- **Connectivity Mapping:** Real-time health monitoring of undersea fiber-optic cables and internet gateways.  
- **Energy Flux:** Tracking of oil/gas pipeline status and power grid stability across manufacturing hubs.  

### 🔬 Contagion Analysis (AI-Powered)  
- **Ollama Integration:** Local LLM synthesis of multi-source trade data.  
- **Risk Propagation:** Predictive modeling of how localized disruptions (e.g., a port strike or natural disaster) cascade through the global economy.  

---

## 🏗 INFRASTRUCTURE & TECH STACK  

- **Core Engine:** HTML5, TypeScript, vanilla CSS.  
- **Globe Rendering:** `globe.gl` + `Three.js` (WebGL).  
- **Flat Mapping:** `deck.gl` + `MapLibre GL`.  
- **Intelligence Pipeline:** Real-time GDELT and RSS news aggregation.  
- **Local AI:** Integrated `Ollama` support for offline/private intelligence synthesis.
